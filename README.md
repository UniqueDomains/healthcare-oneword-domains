## ⚠️ Methodology and caveats

This selection is broad rather than narrow. It spans 16,195 healthcare keyword domains across 505 TLDs, with examples ranging from care.contact and healthy.systems to healing.associates and pharmaceutical.dev. The median ask is 4,064, so pricing sits well above hand-registration territory for many names. When comparing these domains, focus first on extension fit and commercial credibility. A direct healthcare term on a relevant TLD can read clearly, while a mismatched or novelty extension can weaken trust, resale depth, or long-term brand use. For founders, memorability and clarity matter most. For investors, extension quality and realistic resale depth matter first.

- Median ask is 4,064 across this healthcare selection
- 505 TLDs means wide variation in trust and renewal profile
